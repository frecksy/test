The One with the Fansite

How you doin'?

Welcome to my fansite all about the hit 90's television show, Friends. On this site you will be able to learn all about the show, who the characters are, and some of the best episodes. Feel free to explore and read through the site as you wish! I hope you enjoy and thanks for stopping by this part ofgThe Attic!

Friends is a hit American sitcom that aired from 1994 to 2004, created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman. The show is a comedy about six 20-30-year old friends living in the New York City borough of Manhattan. It stars Jennifer Aniston, Courtney Cox, Lisa Kudrow, Matt Le Blanc, Matthew Perry, and David Schwimmer.
